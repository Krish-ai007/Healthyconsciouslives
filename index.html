A BALANCED APPROACH TO NUTRITION

Hello !!! I am Shreeya Zaveri, a dedicated dietitian with a Bachelor of Science in Home Science specializing in Food Science and Nutrition, along with a Master’s degree in Nutrition and Dietetics. With a strong foundation in nutrition and a passion for promoting healthy lifestyles, I am also a diabetes educator. Whether you’re seeking guidance for managing a specific condition or looking to optimize your overall nutrition, I am here to support you on your journey to better health.
